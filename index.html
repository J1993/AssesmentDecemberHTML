Welcome

Chollerton Tearooms welcomes you to its brand new website where you can discover many offers and services that can be adapted to your tastes.

Services

Tearoom

Chollerton Tearooms offers a selection of traditional teas, herbal teas, fruit teas and coffees, as well as juices and smoothies available to enjoy inside or to take away. For those looking for something to eat, pastries, muffins and cakes are made fresh daily while our breakfast/ brunch and daytime menus are packed full of delicious options including Eggs Benedict, Full English Breakfasts, omelettes, sandwiches, salads and quiches that are guaranteed to delight.
